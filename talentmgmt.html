Talent Management

What is Talent Management (TM) at OCIO?

Talent management is a process that helps build sustained excellence in the leadership within the organization. For talent management to be effective, employees must be placed in the positions that optimize their skills and goals. The aim is for everyone to reach and exceed their potential, while developing experiences.

The Community Management Office believes that Talent Management:

  • Helps in Employee's retention
  • Provides support to succession planning for the new generation of executives and
  • Contributes in building a strong Digital community in the GC

Annual Talent Management Cycle at OCIO

The Talent Management (TM) annual cycle place the different arcitivies on a calendar from the data extraction and data collection to the identification of a pool of potential candidates qualified as ready for lateral movement or ready for advancement. The cycle focuses more on the EXposition and the Exend EXposition steps whereas the Referral process and the Digital Employment Equity Mentoring Program are fluid and ongoing throughout the year.

What do we offer?

If you are looking to staff a CIO or an Executive position in the digital community, we are happy to assist in your search with the Referral Process!

Referral Process

Under the new Policy on Service and Digital, organizations are asked to consult with the OCIO in the appointment of a new Chief Information Officer (CIO) or other pivotal digital executive positions.

Our team undertakes ongoing talent management throughout the year. Each December, we solicit and gather a list of candidates from departments at the EX-01 to EX-03 levels who are ready for lateral movement and/or advancement. This is EXposition.

Similarly, we undertake this same process for those at the EX equivalent and EX minus one level in June. This is EXtend EXposition.

Data sources:
We use the latest data available from the Executive Talent Management System (ETMS), from possible recent pools, as well as direct input from organizationsand candidates.

Candidate Application Sharing Procedures:
To ensure the TM team is efficient and doesn't disseminate unnecessary data to the organization, it is essential to first contact the candidates to seek interest for the position.

Each December, we solicit and gather a list of candidates, submitted by Human Resources Advisors, CIOs and/or ADMs from many organizations, at the EX-01 to the EX-03 levels who are ready for Potential interdepartmental opportunities, Learning, Development and networking activities and/or advancement. This roundtable is called EXposition.
