2020 CIO Summit

When: Wednesday, November 25th, 8AM to 1:15PM EST      Where: Microsoft Teams      Attendance: Invitation Only

The Government of Canada is looking forward to hosting the first virtual Chief Information Officer (CIO) Summit. Despite the great changes to how we work and gather, this virtual summit is an opportunity to meet and demonstrate our resilience in the face of unprecedented challenges and how we can build the #GCDigital community of tomorrow.

Agenda   |   Resources   |   Twitter |   |   Contact Us

2020 CIO Summit Agenda

The main theme of the CIO Summit is "Working in #GCDigital" where we will have the opportunity to look back at the efforts made in the past year, discuss the future of digital talent and look forward to collectively implementing the updated Digital Operations Strategic Plan

  • 8:30-8:45AM EST: Informal Meet and Greet

  • 8:45-9:00AM EST: Welcome and Introductions

    Marc Brouillard, Chief Information Officer of Canada

    Join us for a warm welcome to the day’s discussion and events.

  • 9:00-9:15AM EST: Keynote

    Joyce Murray, Minister of Digital Government

    Join us to take a look at the past year and how the Government of Canada can move forward in this critical and unprecedented time where the importance of digital government is bigger than ever.

  • 9:15-10:05AM EST: Digital Operations Strategic Plan 2021-2024: Making Change Real

    Join us as our panel showcases three different departments that have implemented effective strategies

    Moderator:

    Jody Lobb, Treasury Board of Canada Secretariat

    Panelists:

    Jason Reid, Natural Resources Canada

    Vidya Shankarnaravan, Innovation, Science and Economic Development

    Ryan Hum, Canada Energy Regulator

  • 10:05-10:15AM EST: Health Break

    Feedback Survey

  • 10:15-11:10AM EST: Working in #GCDigital: Transformation of Talent

    Join us as our panel showcases three different initiatives that have or are in the process of implementing effective strategies to attract, retain and develop talent, with a focus on building a diverse and inclusive workforce.

    Moderator:

    Shannon Archibald, Shared Services Canada

    Panelists:

    Natasha Clarke, Province of Nova Scotia

    John Baker, D2L

    Stephane Cousineau, Shared Services Canada

  • 11:10AM-12:00PM EST: Breakout sessions focused on the Digital Operations Strategic Plan 2021-2024

    Pillar 1: Services that meet clients’ needs and expectations

    “Making the GC Digital Standards Real”

    Facilitator:

    Sonya Read

    Pillar 2: Technology that is fit for digital services and operations

    “What’s Next in the Journey to the Cloud”

    Facilitator:

    Paul Wagner

    Pillar 3: Operations that enable digital-first delivery

    “Moving from Project to Product”

    Facilitator:

    Denis Skinner

  • 12:00-12:05PM EST: Closing remarks

  • 12:05-12:15PM EST: Health Break

    Feedback Survey

  • 12:15-1:15PM EST: (Optional) Lunch and Learn with Gartner on Product Management
