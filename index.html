IM-IT Functional Community (IFC)

IM/IT Community Management Office

Our mission is to build and promote a robust ecosystem to attract, develop, retain, and recognize a capable and competent IM/IT workforce. We do this by providing support, guidance, products, and services on Strategic Human Resources Management issues to the CIO of Canada, the community of departmental CIOs, and the IM/IT community. We serve the government's largest functional community consisting of over 20,000 computer systems (CS) and other practitioner and leaders

Workplace Management

Fostering a strong sense of community and pride is part of our work. Through out community management initiatives, we provide recognition and leadership across government departments to transform and strengthen digital capacity and services for Canadians.
