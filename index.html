Digital Community

Community Management Office

Our mission is to build and promote a robust ecosystem to attract, develop, retain, and recognize a capable and competent digital/IM-IT workforce. We do this by providing support, guidance, products and services on Strategic Human Resources Management issues to the CIO of Canada, digital executives and departmental CIOs, and the digital/IM-IT community. We serve the government's largest functional community consisting of over 20,000 computer systems (CS) as well as other digital community practitioners and leaders.

Organizational Readiness and Outreach

Providing a strong foundation for effective HR practices and organizational design is paramount for enabling our organizations to deliver on their mandates.

Recruitment

The recruitment program helps to attract and refer quality candidates across the GC. We directly support the IM/IT community, the Chief Information Officer of Canada as the functional head of the IM/IT community and the Policy on Service and Digital.

Talent Management

Developing our digital talent has never been more important. Talent management is a process that helps build sustained excellence in the leadership within the organization.

Career Pathways (coming soon)

Career pathways (under development) will provide a central portal for career development information and resources to support employees and managers in developmental and career progression objectives. These resources are provided to bridge and facilitate career transitions and progression.

Community Management

Fostering a strong sense of community and pride is part of our work. Through our community management initiatives, we provide recognition and leadership across government departments to transform and strengthen digital capacity and services for Canadians.

Community Demographics

Demographics (updates in progress) provide a snapshot of our digital community population, past and present, looking at broad characteristics of age, gender and other data.
